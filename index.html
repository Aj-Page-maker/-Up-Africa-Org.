Who We Are

We believe that refugees and other migrants hold significant economic potential, and that young people in Africa have the ability to support
our continent and planet's needs for the coming generations

Our goal is to unlock the abilities and potential of these people through a long term solution of advanced learning, entrepreneurship
experience and lasting leadership, to create positive change

This is how our first Class in Uganda looks like

We started in Uganda, where we have been able to support over 1000 refugees and young people through our programs. Our focus is on providing advanced learning and entrepreneurship experience to help them thrive.

Mission Statement

Providing educational, entrepreneurial and professional skills to refugees and low-income communities using Information Technology and empowering them through employment to further support education and development within their communities.
